We are creating life-long memories

Our goal is to create happy, lifelong memories for every family that visits. Since 1988, Pojo's Family Fun Center has been a cherished destination in Boise, offering a safe and entertaining environment for families to connect and have fun. Built on a foundation of joy and excitement, we are proud to be a community hub where unforgettable memories are made.

Pojo's Attractions

Attractions

Welcome to Pojo's Family Fun Center, where we bring joy, laughter, and excitement to every visit. From thrilling rides to engaging arcade games, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. Gather your loved ones and make unforgettable memories at Pojo's—a place that feels just like home.

Pojo's Attractions

Indoor Carousel

As Idaho's only indoor carousel, it features handpicked animals representing local wildlife, such as bobcats and whitetail deer.

Bumper Cars

Engage in friendly competition with family and friends on the indoor bumper car arena, a popular attraction since 1994.

Arcade Games

The expansive arcade offers a mix of classic and modern games, including driving simulators and redemption games.

Soft Play Area

Designed for younger children, this area provides a safe and colorful environment with age-appropriate activities.

Carousel Café

The on-site café serves a variety of delicious foods. including pizza, burgers, sandwiches, and kid-friendly meals.

Birthday Party Packages

Pojo's offers customizable packages for birthdays and other events, including reserved seating, food, and game tokens.
